        • SLS Series
          • SLS Series
            SmarAct‘s SLS series is a new range of positioners, accessories and tools that allows you to simply configure your system. The series consists of linear positioners as well as rotary positioners and goniometers to cover all demanding positioning tasks in your laboratory. Due to the consequent compatibility of the hole pattern you can combine different types of positioners and brackets to build multi-axis positioning systems. Adapters for standard metric breadboards (M6 screws, 25 mm hole pattern) extend the compatibility to the range of widely used optomechanical components.

    About SmarAct

    SmarAct is located in Oldenburg (Germany) and was founded in 2005 as a spin-off from the University of Oldenburg. In the last 15 years we have grown to a company with almost 140 employees who cover the complete production process to ensure a high level of product quality including development, construction, mechanical production, electronic production and assembly.
